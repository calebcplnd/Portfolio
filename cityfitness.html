CITYFITNESS

As an in-house Graphic Designer and Marketing Assistant for CityFitness New Zealand I created a variety of design solutions for the brand's digital channels, including social media pages and display ad campaigns. This involved content ideation, production and distribution. I designed static posts for Instagram and Facebook, Advertisement designs for billboards and flyers, and produced, filmed, and edited short-form video content for Instagram and TikTok.
